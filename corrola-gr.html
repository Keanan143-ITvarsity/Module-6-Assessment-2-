Toyota Corolla GR

The Toyota Corolla GR (Gazoo Racing) brings a new level of excitement to the compact car segment, offering sporty performance and aggressive styling. The Corolla GR is powered by a 1.6-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine that produces 300 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, a remarkable output for its class. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, providing an engaging driving experience for enthusiasts. The all-wheel-drive system ensures excellent traction and stability, making the Corolla GR a capable performer on both the track and everyday roads.

In terms of design, the Corolla GR stands out with its bold, aerodynamic bodywork, including a large front grille, flared wheel arches, and a rear spoiler. The interior is equally sporty, featuring supportive sport seats, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and aluminum pedals. Advanced technology is integrated throughout the cabin, including an 8-inch infotainment system with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a premium audio system. Safety features such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure alert, and pre-collision braking come standard. The Toyota Corolla GR delivers a thrilling driving experience while maintaining the practicality and reliability the Corolla is known for.
